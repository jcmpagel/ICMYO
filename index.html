International Coordination Meeting of Youth Organisations (ICMYO)

About ICMYO

The International Coordination Meeting of Youth Organisations (ICMYO) is a global network that unites the world's largest youth movements and regional youth platforms. Officially established in 2004 and further strengthened in 2013, ICMYO aims to represent the diverse voices of youth-led organizations worldwide, fostering coordination and cooperation to enhance their influence on global youth policy processes and to establish full partnerships with relevant stakeholders.

Objectives

  • Represent the diverse voices of youth-led organizations globally.
  • Facilitate cooperation among youth-led organizations at regional and global levels.
  • Coordinate political inputs to global youth policy.

Activities

ICMYO convenes annually in a meeting organized by a task force elected by its members. These meetings serve as a platform for member organizations to coordinate their activities, share insights, and develop unified strategies to influence global youth policies effectively.
